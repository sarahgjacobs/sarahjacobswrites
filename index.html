About Sarah Jacobs

Sarah is an avid story teller who seeks to bring to life the little worlds inside her head. Already on her fourth book, Sarah primarily writes speculative fiction ranging anywhere from high-tech stories to ancient fighting states. If she's not writing her next story, she's most likely petting her dog, watching cartoons, trying a new hobby, going for a run, taking a nap, or programming this website. Stay tuned to see what Sarah does next!

Sarah is currently writing:

Advice for Killers

Sci-fi, Action, Drama, Romance

AFK is a riveting, high-class story about the illusive SecTech companies that guard the nation's darkest secrets, and the people who run them. The story begins with the death of Securos' CEO--a murder, it seems. Though no evidence arises. Peter, the next in line to inherit Securos, is pulled from his perfect world far from technology and schemes, and though he doesn't desire the reigns of Securos' billion-dollar clients, he must accept the race ahead of him if he wants to keep the nation's security out of greedy hands. But choosing Securos might mean pulling the love of his life into the deadly climb to the top. In the end, only one can stand.

Sarah's Completed Books:

Commendation for Reforia - 2022

Historical Fiction, Action, Adventure, Romance

The Flat Shores was a notoriously dark land for the worst of the criminals to live locked away forever, but Kel isn't one of the criminals--her parents were before they died. The only way for her to escape her cruel upbringing was to recieve a pass from a Consul, one of the few highly qualified fighters of the land. But it isn't every day such an esteemed fighter dares to walk into the Flat Shores.
Evil arrives one morning with intentions to gather as many abled bodies as it can, and Kel happens to be swept up into his palm. Greedy for loyal soldies, Synth uses his charisma to win over the children like Kel, children that inherited the strength of their parents but had no formal training in order to weild it. Knowing her fate was beginning to darken under Synth's training, Kel escapes with her quick wits and the trust of a local Headmaster. Unable to help her more than that, the Headmaster sends her to Hollowfall where one of Reforia's top six Consuls, Keystone, has an entire organization of spys set up in order to gether intellegence on Synth. Keystone is a man of little faith or sympathy for his students, escpecially toward Kel who is stubborn, hot headed, and worst of all, connected to Synth.
Under Keystone's tough regimen, Kel will be tested to the edge of her abilities until it's finally time for Synth to return to claim what's his. History will unfold once again as they states come head to head. Will Keystone trust Kel enough, will her strength fail her, or will death find them all first?

The Circuit - 2021

Sci-Fi, Action, Romance

Thriving behind wires and computer screens on the electric campus of Cadella Premiere University (CPU), Meili Hane seeks to become the best programmer of her generation, not for the fame and glory, but to do what police never did: find and defeat the illusive programmers who caused her father's accident.
As Meili struggles to follow each meticulous law of the programming world, news of an underground group of hackers famously known as The Circuit bend the laws in order to shatter harmful companies, incriminate enemies on the loose, and go where no other programmer can reach them...a place Meili needs to go. During a high-stakes competition, Meili's unique and dark competition style draws the attention of The Circuit's leader, Issac Young, but a more dangerous opponent enters the game, and he has eyes for Meili's skills as well.
Peter Glascow, long time best friend and rival of Meili Hane's father, is engrossed by the untouchable power that programming brings, and his desires multiply when he sees Meili's inherited skills. GreaTech, Glascow's informibable organization of programmers, seeks to create an empire of technology that will monopolize the city and the very resources Meili's dad needs to survive. Meili is pressed hard between the police that chase close at her heels and the programmers that threaten to shut her down. When at last her pursures close in, Meili will have to hide even from the ones she loves most in order to save her family. But when she returns, she'll be the virus that destroyes them all.
